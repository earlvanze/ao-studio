FAQ

What exactly do I receive?

A concise report showing where your business can improve response times, organization, follow-up, and routine operations first.

How fast is turnaround?

Typical delivery is 1–2 business days after successful checkout and file submission.

What happens after I submit?

Your intake details are saved, your file is uploaded securely, and Stripe handles payment. After payment, Earl reviews the material and delivers the audit. If you want to talk first, the main consult path is Calendly.com/earlco.

Who gets notified?

Right now the funnel is database-backed, not email-notification-led. Inquiry and audit details are captured in Supabase and scheduling happens through Calendly. There is no plain email notification path wired on this site today.

How do you tailor the audit?

We usually anchor it to the owner priority that matters most: more freedom, better service, better systems, more trust, or more measurable control and results.

Can this turn into a full setup?

Yes. If the fit is clear, the audit becomes a simple first step into the full implementation package.
