Pricing and model-cost clarity

$500/month is the service layer

This monthly fee covers Earl’s management layer: setup support, maintenance, ongoing improvements, and keeping the system running well. Earl covers server costs inside that fee.

The business pays for the AI model

The AI model itself is a separate cost paid by the client. Depending on your needs, that may be ChatGPT Plus or Pro, Claude Code Pro or Max, or Gemini.

Sometimes the higher cost is only during setup

Some builds need a stronger model only for the first month during setup, around $200, and then can move to something closer to $20/month after that depending on the workflow.

Local models are also possible

If you prefer more local control, we can design around local AI models too. In that case, the client provides the hardware, such as a Mac Mini or another machine that fits the budget.

Clear costs, not hidden bundles

This keeps the pricing honest. Earl handles the operating side. The client pays for the AI model they choose. You can see where the money is going.

We help choose the right fit

On the consult, we recommend the likely model setup and expected monthly cost based on what you actually want the system to do.
